Economic Development

Agriculture

Agriculture has been the most effective form of earning a living in the early days till current. Subsistence farming is the most commonly used form of agriculture. The agriculture products are being sold for money while others are reserved for consumption. There has been a number of logging and agriculture projects but the impact on the local economy is minimal, most of them went failed. A ward meeting late last year resolved to accept for an application of an AGRICULTURE TA to convert a 50 hectares of land into an agriculture area. The benefits generated from this project will help to support the community on its financial needs in terms of infrastructure projects and individual benefits.

There are specific portions of land being alienated which is owned by the state. Others have not been properly acquired and still yet to be confirmed. With the current increased number of logging operations around these areas, the land owners through a local landowner company had signed an agreement with a developer to lease a portion of land for economic benefits. These benefits were used to secure land issues. A ward meeting late last year also resolved to support the respective ILGs to apply for registrations over this area to secure from land grabs.

2.Others

There are other potential resources that can be utilised to generate opportunities that will support the community (people) but will need more resources and effort. Transportation and Market avenues are still an existing problem that are being faced with. That is why, most of our cash crops such as cocoa and coconuts are left unattended to, not even processed for marketing. The following needs resources and training;

  • Livestock
  • Fishery
  • Tourism
  • Other Cash crops
