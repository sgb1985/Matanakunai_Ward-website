Law and Order

Village Court

The ward has an established village court system and also mandated village court officials approved by the department of Justice and Attorney General. It oversees law and order problems in both Matanakunai and Open Bay Wards. Officials are selected from within the two wards. There is a village court facility (Court House) but is no longer usable due to its bad condition needing intensive renovation. All court hearings are done at the Open Bay rural police station.

Ward Law and Order Committee

A law and order committee was formed in 2017 and is still current but we have some of the committees moved out to other provinces and places. This law and order committee comprises of police personnel and appointed committees from Open Bay and Matanakunai wards. A meeting has already been sat for to restructure the Ward Law & Order Committees.
