Welcome to Matanakunai Ward Information Site

Matanakunai Ward aims to establish an environment that is conducive and safe for a better livelihood and promote partnership, transparency, accountability, and individual ownership of its land and resources. It will ensure that basic necessities are set as first priorities in order to meet individual needs and wants and to encourage more sustainable developments into the concern areas and the community as a whole.

In our mere effort to achieve our vision, Matanakunai Ward, through the Ward Development Committees and with the support of the community and other stake-holders, seek to improve its performance through;

1. Economic Development.

Due to a lot of problems faced in terms of service delivery, the ward is looking forward to engage companies and other business houses that intend to use the land for economic activities. Benefits obtained from these projects will be equally distributed among individual land owners through community projects or individual benefits within their own ILGs.

2. Basic Government Services.
